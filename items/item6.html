上海宝钢冶金厂杯子

展品简介

尺寸： 12cm × 11cm（直径）
李佳雯借展，在上世纪80年代，宝钢高炉工程是中国现代化工业的重要里程碑。爷爷和他的同事们，夜以继日地奋战在建设第一线，克服了无数技术难题和艰苦条件。这个杯子，是工程竣工时颁发的纪念品，上面刻着“宝钢高炉工程建成纪念”的字样。每次看到它，爷爷的眼中都会闪烁着自豪的光芒。他总是说，那是一个激情燃烧的岁月，每个人都为了国家的工业发展而努力。这个杯子，不仅是对爷爷工作的肯定，更是那个时代精神的象征。随着时间的流逝，这个杯子虽然表面有些磨损，但它所承载的故事和情感却愈发珍贵。它见证了爷爷的青春，也见证了中国工业的发展。现在，我想把它作为展品，让更多的人了解那段历史，感受那个时代人们的热情和奉献。这只杯子远超其实物的价值，小时候，每当夜幕降临，爷爷总会从橱柜的最深处拿出这只杯子，慢慢注入他最爱的老茶，伴随着袅袅升起的茶香，开始了我们的故事时光。那是爷爷在宝钢工作的岁月，是他青春与汗水挥洒的地方，他说，这座高炉就像他的孩子，从奠基到竣工，每一个螺丝钉、每一寸钢铁，都刻印着他和工友们的名字。而这只杯子，就是那段峥嵘岁月的最好证明，它见证了爷爷和他的工友们日复一日、年复一年的坚守与奉献。记得有一次，我问爷爷为什么会对这只看起来有些陈旧的杯子如此珍视？爷爷笑着告诉我，这只杯子背后的意义非凡，它不仅代表着宝钢的历史，更代表了那个时代人们对于理想的追求和国家富强的梦想。每当我看到这只杯子，就好像看到了那个年代人们的热血沸腾和对未来的无限憧憬。这不是一只普通的杯子，它是时光的容器，装载着一段段滚烫的记忆。这是宝钢为庆祝高炉工程竣工所特别定制的纪念品，采用高炉钢材精心铸就，它见证了我国钢铁业的辉煌时刻，承载着劳动者辛勤付出的心血与自豪。每一代人都有自己的使命和责任，而我们也应该为了实现梦想而不懈努力。希望通过这次特展，让更多人听到这个杯子背后的故事，感受到那份跨越时间的情感和力量。现在，我愿意将这只杯子的故事分享出来，希望更多人能够感受到它背后承载的历史与情感，也希望每个人都能从这段故事中汲取力量，珍惜眼前的每一份劳动与付出，勇敢追梦，不负韶华。

Shanghai Baosteel Metallurgical Plant Cup

Exhibit Overview

Dimensions: 12cm × 11cm (diameter)
Lent by Li Jiawen, this cup commemorates the Baosteel Blast Furnace Project, a significant milestone in China's modern industrial history during the 1980s. My grandfather and his colleagues worked tirelessly on the front lines, overcoming countless technical challenges and harsh conditions. This cup, awarded upon the project's completion, is inscribed with "Baosteel Blast Furnace Project Completion Commemoration." Every time my grandfather saw it, his eyes would gleam with pride. He often said those were passionate years when everyone worked toward the nation's industrial progress. This cup is not only a recognition of his efforts but also a symbol of that era's spirit. Though its surface has worn over time, the stories and emotions it carries grow ever more precious. It witnessed my grandfather's youth and China's industrial rise. Now, I wish to share it as an exhibit to let more people learn about that history and feel the passion and dedication of that time. This cup holds far more than its material value. As a child, whenever night fell, my grandfather would take it from the deepest part of the cabinet, pour his favorite old tea, and with the rising aroma, begin our story time. Those were his years at Baosteel, where he poured his youth and sweat. He said the furnace was like his child—from foundation to completion, every screw and every inch of steel bore his and his colleagues' names. This cup is the best testament to those remarkable years, bearing witness to their daily and yearly perseverance and devotion. Once, I asked why he cherished this seemingly old cup so much. With a smile, he told me its profound significance—it represents not just Baosteel's history but also the era's pursuit of ideals and dreams of national prosperity. Seeing this cup feels like glimpsing the fervor and boundless hope of that time. It is no ordinary cup; it is a vessel of time, holding scalding memories. Crafted from furnace steel to celebrate the project's completion, it marks a glorious moment in China's steel industry, carrying the workers' toil and pride. Each generation has its mission and responsibility, and we, too, should strive for our dreams. I hope this exhibition lets more people hear the cup's story and feel its timeless emotion and strength. I share this cup's story now, hoping others can sense the history and emotions it holds, drawing strength from it to value every effort, chase dreams boldly, and live fully.
